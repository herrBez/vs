{
 This is the solution of the exercise and is pretty cool.
  \begin{align*}
  \B{\syntrue} = \semtrue \\ \\
  \B{\synfalse} = \semfalse \\ \\
  \B{a_1 = a_2} = \caseFun{\semtrue}{\A{a_1} = \A{a_2}}{\semfalse}{\A{a_1} \neq 
\A{a_2}} \\ \\
  \end{align*}
